Introduction
 
      
 
        The rapid growth of the gaming industry has led to an increase in the number of
         individuals spending extended hours engaged in gaming activities. While this 
         growth has created new opportunities in entertainment, esports, and content 
         creation, it has also raised concerns regarding the physical health and 
         well-being of gamers, particularly in relation to posture, fatigue, and 
         long-term musculoskeletal issues.

     

        

        ErgoPlay Gaming is a proposed company that aims to address these challenges 
        by providing ergonomically designed gaming equipment that enhances both comfort
         and performance. This website proposal outlines the development of an online platform
          that will serve as a central hub for showcasing products, educating users on 
          ergonomic practices, and building a strong digital presence for the brand.

        

    The purpose of this proposal is to present the design, functionality, and technical 
        requirements of the ErgoPlay Gaming website, with the goal of improving user experience,
         increasing online sales, and promoting awareness of healthy gaming habits.
        

        ErgoPlay Gaming is a fictional company founded in 2026 that specializes in ergonomic
         gaming equipment designed to improve comfort, posture, and performance. The company
         focuses on gamers who spend long hours playing and need health-conscious solutions.
         Products include ergonomic chairs, keyboards, mice, and desk setups.
